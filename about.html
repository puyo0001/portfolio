About

María José Puyol is a Chilean entrepreneur, graphic designer, and illustrator deeply passionate about visual communication and art. With a bachelor’s degree in Design, she has honed her skills in Illustration, creating innovative graphics and compelling visuals.

Currently based in Ottawa, Canada, María José is dedicating to enrich her expertise in Graphic Design and Web Development. She actively contributes her creativity and dedication to various volunteer and freelance projects, reflecting her proactive approach and commitment to excellence.
